About Me

Let me introduce myself

Hi, I'm Jon. I own three demanding cats. In my spare time I try to play guitar and develop my photography skills.

I graduated with my Ph.D. in Mechanical Engineering from UC Davis in early 2022. I really enjoy modeling and simulating mechatronic systems along with other energy domains with bond graphs.

I started out with Matlab/Simulink for all my simulation needs. But noticing that many jobs ask for a more diverse set of programming languages, I started to implement Python for my needs. My site and github aims to document my growth.

My interests include modeling, simulation, and controls
